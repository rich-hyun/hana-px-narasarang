Hana2.0 font files are intentionally not included in this package. Copy Hana2-Regular.ttf, Hana2-Medium.ttf, Hana2-Bold.ttf, Hana2-Heavy.ttf from your Hana2.0_TTF.zip into this folder before Netlify upload if you want the official Hana font to load. The site works with fallback Korean fonts if you skip this step.
